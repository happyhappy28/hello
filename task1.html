VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball is a popular team sport played by two teams of six players each, separated by a net. The main objective is to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent's court. It requires skills like serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and defending. Volleyball is played indoors and on the beach and is an Olympic sport loved by many around the world.

JAPANESE VOLLEYBALL TEAM

The Japanese volleyball team is known for its discipline, speed, and strong defense. The women's team won gold in the 1964 Tokyo Olympics. Japan focuses on teamwork, fast plays, and precise techniques. Players like Yuki Ishikawa and Mayu Ishikawa are well-known internationally. The team often competes in world events and is respected globally.
